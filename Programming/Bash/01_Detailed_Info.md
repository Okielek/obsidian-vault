## `rm` 
### Important flags
* `-i` — interactive (asks before deleting)
* `-r` — recursive (for directories)
* `-f` — force (no prompts)
* `-rf` — force and recursive **dangerous!** 
## `ls`
### Important flags
* `-a` — shows all files (including hidden such as *.file.txt*)
## `cd`
### Important flags
* `-` — last open directory
